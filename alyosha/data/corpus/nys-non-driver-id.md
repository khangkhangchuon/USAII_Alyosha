# How to get a New York State non-driver ID

A NY State non-driver photo ID is required for almost everything — banking,
employment, benefits applications. You apply in person at any NY DMV office.

## Who qualifies

A person with lawful status in the US, of any age, can get a non-driver ID card.

### Special cases

- **Under 21:** a parent or guardian must verify your identity by completing
  the Statement of Identity by Parent/Guardian form (**MV-45**) and signing it
  at the DMV with a representative witnessing. They must present 6 points of
  proof of their own identity.
- **Children under 16:** a parent applies on behalf of the child using MV-45,
  the child's Social Security card (or proof of ineligibility), and proof of
  date of birth.

## Step 1 — get pre-screened

Use the DMV's interactive pre-screening tool to find out exactly which
documents you need to bring:
<https://eservices.dmv.ny.gov/TAP/eServices/?Link=ReqResp.ESV.PREPSTRT>

Or review the complete list of accepted documents in form **ID-44** ("How to
Apply for A New York Learner Permit, Driver License, Non-Driver ID Card"):
<https://dmv.ny.gov/forms/id44.pdf>

## Step 2 — fill out form MV-44

The application form is **MV-44 — Application for Permit, Driver License or
Non-Driver ID Card**: <https://dmv.ny.gov/forms/mv44.pdf>

## Step 3 — visit a DMV office

Apply in person with your completed forms and supporting documents. The DMV
issues a temporary non-photo document valid for **60 days** at the appointment.

## Fees and fee waivers

Fee details: <https://dmv.ny.gov/non-driver-id/non-driver-id-fees-and-refunds>

You may be eligible for a **reduced fee or no-fee** non-driver ID if:

- You are 62 or older and receive Supplemental Security Income, **or**
- You are any age and receive public assistance.

To get a reduced or no-fee ID, you must apply **by mail or at a DMV office**.
You cannot apply online for the fee waiver.

## Timeline

Your ID will arrive in the mail. You can check mailing status at
<https://dmv.ny.gov/node/8911>. Processing may be delayed during high renewal
periods.
