# SNAP food benefits in New York City

SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program) provides monthly food benefits.
Apply through the City of New York's ACCESS HRA system.

## Eligibility

The City evaluates:
- How much money you earn
- How much money you receive from benefits
- How many people are in your household

Depending on your situation, you may be eligible for SNAP even if you're not a
U.S. citizen.

## Timeline

If you are eligible, you will receive your benefits within **30 days** of
applying. You will need to recertify for SNAP once every year.

## How to apply

You can apply for **Cash Assistance, SNAP, and Medicaid all at the same time**
through ACCESS HRA.

### Online (fastest)

1. Go to <https://a069-access.nyc.gov/ACCESSNYC/application.do>.
2. Complete the application.
3. Complete an eligibility interview by phone.
4. Track progress in ACCESS HRA.

### By phone or in person

- Get a paper application from any SNAP Center, a community-based partner
  organization, or by downloading
  <https://otda.ny.gov/programs/applications/4826.pdf>.
- Call 718-557-1399 (HRA Infoline) for help.
- Mail completed applications to:
  Division of SNAP Services, Mail Application & Referral Unit (MARU),
  P.O. Box 24510, Brooklyn, NY 11201.
- Or submit in person at any SNAP Center or partner organization.
